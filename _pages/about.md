Gilian Ponte is a Marketing Ph.D student at the Faculty of Business and Economics, Rijksuniversiteit Groningen (The Netherlands). He holds a M.Sc degree in Marketing and a Research Master with a focus on artificial intelligence and applied econometrics from the Rijksuniversiteit Groningen. My research interests lie in Marketing, Artificial Intelligence and Econometrics. My current research focuses on the development of methodologies to combat privacy and fairness issues in marketing. Previously, he worked for two years as a web analyst at Blokker Holding.
